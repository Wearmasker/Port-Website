GAME AUDIO
(2025–2026)

Viral Impact — a serious roguelike for 's Heeren Loo

Sound Designer · Audio Implementer · Unity · FMOD · Ableton Live · Serum · Client: 's Heeren Loo · 2025

Viral Impact is a serious roguelike developed in Unity for 's Heeren Loo, designed to help players with mild intellectual disabilities (MID) practice safe and appropriate online communication through short combat levels followed by conversation-driven learning moments. I built the game's audio identity from the ground up — designing SFX (player, UI, enemies, environment) with layering, processing, and Serum synthesis; composing adaptive music for hub and battle states; and building world-specific ambience for the WhatsApp, Instagram, and Scam worlds. I also implemented all audio using FMOD + Unity, including event logic, parameters, and a centralized AudioManager to streamline integration and iteration.
