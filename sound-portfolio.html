PROFESSIONAL HIGHLIGHTS

Below is a 6 months project where I was the sound designer and the actor at the same time. This project was too promote Münster, showing some location that tourist can visit that is usually hidden or not easily found on travel website and tourist guide.

LOFI BGM (2024)

In 2024, during one of my project I wanted to learn more about different kind of music as well as how to evoke different emotion, for this composition I try to go for a lofi genre combine with C Minor, to create a chill and also sad vibe for days when you feel down and need to be alone.

EPIC ORCHESTRAL (2024)

In 2024, during the same project as LOFI BGM , for this composition I try to go for a orchestral genre combine with C Major, to bring out a fantasy theme when the character discover a whole new world and convey their feeling as well as give player context of how marvel the world is.

HAPPY AND INNOCENT (2024)

In 2024, during the same project as LOFI BGM , for this composition I try to go for a orchestral genre combine with C Major, I want to test out different kind of instrument that bring a joyful melodies.
