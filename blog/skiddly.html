SKIDDLY

Sound design and game design contributions for an educational mobile game teaching kids about sustainable development.

Role Sound Designer
Also Game Designer
Format Mobile Game
Audience Ages 8–15
Team Group project
Year 2023

THE BRIEF

Skiddly is an educational mobile game built by my team to teach kids (ages 8–15) about sustainable development concepts — energy conservation, renewable energy, and how those concepts show up in everyday life — through a fun, easy-to-understand game.

THE APPROACH

Designing audio for kids meant prioritising clarity and warmth — every sound should feel friendly, never punishing. I aimed for [TONAL TARGETS — bouncy, organic, soft synth-led] and avoided sharp transients so younger players wouldn't get fatigued. Key references: [GAMES YOU REFERENCED].
